About Us

Space Finder is a modern project in field of computer vision (Video processing).In this project the problem is to check in a video (camera) whether empty spaces and occupied spaces in the video are available in the Parking Spaces.

Our goal is to detect the objects( Free, and Occupied spaces in real time)in the video that are available in the Parking Spaces.Secondly the task is to classify the vehciles on the bases of their category.
